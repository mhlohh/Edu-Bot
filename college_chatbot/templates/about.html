About EduGuide AI

EduGuide AI helps students discover the right college and course in India with a powerful filter system, AI-powered suggestions (via Gemini), and clean, ranked results based on NIRF/rating. You can filter by location, stream/course, and budget, then jump straight to each college’s official website or Google Maps location. Whether you’re targeting CSE in Kochi under a certain fee, checking AI & DS fees in Trivandrum, or listing top B.Tech colleges in Tamil Nadu, EduGuide AI surfaces accurate, structured results quickly. Our dataset is organized for stable, fast lookups—no page reloads required—and enhanced with an AI fallback to suggest nearby or relevant colleges when an exact match isn’t available.

Pro tip: On the results list, click the college name to open its official site, or the Map link to view its location instantly.
